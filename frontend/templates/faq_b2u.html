Buy-to-Unglue (Legacy Program)

This program is no longer active. Ebooks unglued through Buy-to-Unglue campaigns remain available in our catalog.
What was Buy-to-Unglue?
Buy-to-Unglue was a program where every ebook copy sold moved a book's ungluing date closer to the present. When the funding goal was met, the ebook was released under a Creative Commons license. The program ran from 2014 to 2025.
